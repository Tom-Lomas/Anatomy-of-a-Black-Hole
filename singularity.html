The singularity of a black hole is the center of it. As of right now, the singularity of a black hole is purely theoretical. The theory proposes that the singularity is a point where all matter that falls into a black hole is crushed to almost infinite density. This will lead to an infinite curvature of space-time. The infinite curvature of space-time means that a black hole will contain an infinite amount of space-time in any direction.
